The real problem

Four things every IPS student and teacher has felt.

Not an imported idea — every feature here answers a specific, lived pain point on this campus.

01

Warnings arrive too late

Students don't get flagged as at-risk until results are already out — long after there was time to fix anything.

02

Placement-readiness is a mystery

Most students only find out how prepared they are for placements in final year, when it's far too late to close the gaps.

03

Teachers get no honest signal

There's no structured, safe channel for teachers to hear how their classes are actually landing — so improvement is guesswork.

04

HODs fly blind until semester-end

Department heads have no early way to spot a struggling class or a teaching gap before it shows up in final results.

One line, two engines

A platform that improves both sides of education — without blaming anyone.

Most hackathon teams build for students only. We build for the whole ecosystem — the single biggest reason this is different.

📉

Risk Score

Calculated live from attendance %, internal marks, and assignment submissions. Falling on all three flags a student "High Risk" — shown on login, before it's too late.

🎯

Placement Readiness Score

Built from a student's skills, projects, and certifications. A single honest percentage — like "68% Placement Ready" — so nobody finds out in final year.

✅

Personalised Improvement Suggestions

Concrete next steps, not vague advice — "Practice coding 1hr/day," "Attend the next placement workshop," "Improve attendance in Subject X."
